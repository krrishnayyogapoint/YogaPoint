Our Story

Welcome to Krrishnay Yoga Point

Founded in 2015, Krrishnay Yoga Point has been dedicated to spreading awareness about the transformative power of yoga in modern lifestyles. Our center is more than just a place to practice yoga - it's a community that nurtures physical, mental, and spiritual growth.

Our mission is to make authentic yoga accessible to everyone, regardless of age, experience level, or background. We combine traditional yogic wisdom with contemporary teaching methods to create a balanced approach to wellness.

The name "Krrishnay" represents the cosmic consciousness and infinite potential within each individual - a philosophy that guides all our teachings.
